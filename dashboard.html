The Through-Line

The Consistent Pattern

In every race effort — 2017, 2018, 2023, 2024, London 2026 — you hit a wall between miles 16 and 19. It moves later as fitness improves. London pushed it to 18. Chicago needs to push it past 20. That only happens with higher mileage, longer long runs, and more MP work in training. (Chicago 2025 is excluded — that was a Team 2 fun run, not a race effort.)

What's Actually Working

FFRT structure is building real speed — the Shamrock Shuffle PR and Soldier Field 10 both show aerobic power is strong. The Tuesday mid-week long run addition is the smart new variable for Chicago. Your half times are competitive; the marathon just needs the muscular endurance to match.
